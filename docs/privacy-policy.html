This Privacy Policy describes how Tide Runner ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about what data is accessed and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). No account creation or login is required to use the App.

1.1 Location Data

The App requests access to your device's precise location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) and approximate location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) solely to:

  • Center the map on your current position, and
  • Fetch marine and weather forecast data relevant to your location.

Location coordinates are sent directly to third-party weather and tide APIs (see Section 3) as query parameters. We do not store, log, or share your location history, and location is never collected while the App is in the background.

1.2 Device & Usage Data

We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, crash telemetry, usage analytics, or any other diagnostic data. No analytics SDK is integrated into the App.

1.3 Local Cache

Marine forecast data fetched from external APIs is cached locally on your device using a Room database to reduce unnecessary network requests and improve performance. This cached data contains only forecast values (e.g. wave height, sea temperature, tide levels) associated with a geographic coordinate. It is stored entirely on-device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.
